# Privacy Policy for The Daily 10



**Last updated:** 2026-01-26



Daily 10 (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is an educational mobile application designed to provide math tutoring for high school students. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Daily 10 app.



## Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the core functionality of the app. This includes:

* **Personal Information**

  * Name
  * Email address
  * Mobile phone number (optional)
  * Date of birth

* **Authentication Information**

  * All data is **securely transferred** with HTTPS.
  * Passwords are securely hashed and are **never stored in plaintext**.

We do **not** collect:

* Device information
* Crash or diagnostic data
* Location data
* User-generated content
* Any other data beyond what is listed above



## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

* Authenticate users
* Provide access to the Daily 10 app
* Deliver relevant math questions and educational content
* Manage user accounts

We do not use or sell your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.



## User Rights

You have the right to:

* Request access to your personal data
* Request corrections to inaccurate data
* Request deletion of your data

To make a request, please contact us at [admin@daily10.com.au](mailto:admin@daily10.com.au).



## Children’s Privacy

Daily 10 is **not intended for children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us and we will promptly delete it.



## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.



## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at [admin@daily10.com.au](mailto:admin@daily10.com.au).
